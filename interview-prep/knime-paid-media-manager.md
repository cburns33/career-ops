### 2. Analytics & Optimization
**Question**: "How do you use data to optimize campaigns beyond standard KPIs?"

**Your Approach**:

- **Conversion Path Analysis**: Identify drop-off points and optimization opportunities

**Question**: "How do you balance standardization vs localization in global campaigns?"

**Your Framework**:
- **Standardized Elements**: Brand messaging, core value propositions, tracking setup

- **Learning**: Applied framework to future campaigns
